AUTHOR GUIDELINES

We have provided the IJMESRT journal template in our site. Prepare the manuscript based on the template format and share with us . If you got any trouble with the template feel free to connect with us via Email.

The submitted manuscript was strictly evaluated by our editorial team and the progress will be updated simultaneously on our site. You can check the progress by using your Article number provided by the time of submission.standard review process shall take 7 - 10 days time. If any corrections needed, it will be updated in the progress section.

After the paper is approved by the editorial board, it will be published within specified time.
